Our Heritage

A Legacy of Taste Since 1995

What started as a small family kitchen has blossomed into Kolkata's most beloved premium dining destination. Our mission has always been simple: to preserve the authenticity of traditional Bengali recipes while presenting them with modern elegance.

Every spice is hand-ground, every ingredient is locally sourced, and every dish is prepared with the same love and warmth you would find in a Bengali home.
